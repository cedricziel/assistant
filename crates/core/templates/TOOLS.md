# Tools — Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff
that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- SSH hosts and aliases
- Device names and locations
- Preferred voices for TTS
- API endpoints or tokens (careful — don't store raw secrets here)
- Anything environment-specific that helps you do your job

## Examples

```markdown
### SSH

- home-server → 192.168.1.100, user: admin

### Devices

- living-room-speaker → Sonos in main area
- desk-light → Hue "Desk Lamp", warm white preferred

### TTS

- Preferred voice: "Nova" (warm, slightly British)
```

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update
skills without losing your notes, and share skills without leaking your
infrastructure.
